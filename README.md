### United States of America Legislative Tracker: Legi-Scan Search

**Bridging the gap between complex legislation and voter understanding.**

Navigating state legislatures can be a daunting task for the average voter. Bills are often hundreds of pages long, filled with "legalese," and structured in ways that obscure their actual impact on the community. 

**Legislative Tracker** is designed to democratize access to legislative data. By combining the **LegiScan API** with **Google Gemini AI**, this tool identifies relevant bills and translates them into clear, concise, and actionable summaries.

---

### Features

*   **Real-Time LegiScan Integration**: Pulls the latest bill data directly from official legislative records.
*   **AI-Powered Clarity**: Uses advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) to strip away jargon and explain what the bill actually does.
*   **Voter-Centric Focus**: Specifically tuned to track issues like social housing, democratic reform (Ranked Choice Voting), and public health.
*   **Dual Search Capability**: Includes an internal pipeline for summaries and a direct LegiScan link for deep-dive research.

---

### How It Works

1.  **Connect**: Input your LegiScan API key to fetch data and a Gemini API key to power the summaries.
2.  **Filter**: Use the pre-configured California-specific search query or enter your own keywords.
3.  **Summarize**: Click "Scan & Summarize" to have the AI provide a breakdown focused on impact rather than just procedure.
4.  **Stay Informed**: Use the results limit to get a digestible digest of the most important recent activity.

---

### Configuration

**LegiScan Search Logic**
The default query is optimized for **California (CA)** and filters for bills that have **passed**. It focuses on several key areas:
*   **Housing**: Social Housing and the CA Housing Authority.
*   **Democracy**: Ranked Choice Voting and Independent Redistricting.
*   **Public Goods**: State Banks, Generic Drug manufacturing, and Public Medicine.
*   **Social Wellbeing**: Loneliness, Community Centers, and Social Infrastructure.

**AI Models**
Users can select between various Gemini models depending on the desired speed and depth of the summary.

---

### Why This Matters
Voter suppression can happen when the law is too complicated for the people to understand. By using AI to summarize legislation, this tool aims to:
*   **Increase Transparency**: Understand what is being voted on before it becomes law.
*   **Save Time**: Get the essential information from a long bill in seconds.
*   **Empower Advocacy**: Provide citizens with the facts they need to communicate with representatives.

---

### Disclaimer
*This tool uses AI to summarize text. While AI is highly capable, summaries should be used as a starting point for understanding. Users are encouraged to click the "View on LegiScan" button to read the full, official text of any bill before taking legislative action.*
